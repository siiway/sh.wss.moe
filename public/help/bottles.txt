Bottles Flatpak Installation & Permissions

Usage:
  curl https://sh.wss.moe/bottles | bash

Features:
  - Installs Bottles from Flathub if not present
  - Applies essential overrides for:
    - Desktop entries (xdg-data/applications)
    - Native Steam integration (~/.local/share/Steam)
    - Flatpak Steam integration (~/.var/app/com.valvesoftware.Steam/data/Steam)
  - User-level overrides (no sudo needed after flatpak install)

No sudo required (except if flatpak not installed)
Pre-dependency: flatpak
Ref: https://wyf9.top/p/linux-newinst#bottles
